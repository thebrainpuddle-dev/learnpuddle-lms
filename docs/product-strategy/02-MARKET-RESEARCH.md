## 7. Regulatory Tailwinds

### NEP 2020 (National Education Policy)

- Mandates technology integration at all education levels
- Created NETF for tech-education integration
- Academic Bank of Credits for cross-institution portability
- Continuous professional development requirement for teachers

### iGOT Karmayogi

- Mandatory online training for ALL central government employees
- Linked to Annual Performance Appraisal Reports (APAR)
- 2,400+ courses in 16 languages
- PSUs/autonomous bodies encouraged to adopt

### Viksit Bharat Shiksha Adhishthan Bill (2025)

- Merging UGC, AICTE, NCTE into single regulatory body
- Unified compliance requirements = opportunity for standardized LMS
