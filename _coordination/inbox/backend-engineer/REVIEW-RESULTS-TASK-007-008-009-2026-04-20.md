  Needs a dedicated `backend/tests/test_exception_handler.py` covering
  401/403/404/405 (renamed to `error`), serializer errors (unchanged),

  fallback. **Recommended fix:** have the handler temporarily emit BOTH
  keys (`{"error": "...", "detail": "..."}`) during the transition, then

  options listed in the review.
